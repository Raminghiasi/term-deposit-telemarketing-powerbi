# Term Deposit Telemarketing – Power BI Business Case

## Objective
Analyze the effectiveness of a telemarketing campaign for term deposit subscriptions and identify high-propensity customer segments to improve targeting efficiency and return on investment.

## Dashboard Overview
The Power BI report consists of two pages:

### 1. Overview (Executive Summary)
- Campaign success rate and volume KPIs
- Conversion performance by key demographics
- Impact of existing banking products on conversion
- Operational efficiency metrics (conversions per 1,000 calls)
- Executive summary with dynamic insights

### 2. Details (Diagnostics & Data Quality)
- Segment-level performance analysis
- Effect of prior campaign outcomes
- Contact fatigue analysis
- Data quality indicators highlighting unknown or incomplete attributes

## Key Insights
- Overall campaign conversion rate is approximately 11.7%
- Customers with prior campaign success and no active loans convert at significantly higher rates
- Conversion effectiveness declines with repeated contact attempts
- Data quality issues, particularly around previous campaign outcomes, materially impact targeting accuracy

## Files
- `Term_Deposit_Telemarketing.pbix` – Interactive Power BI report
- `Term_Deposit_Telemarketing.pdf` – Static export for review

## Notes
- Contact duration is excluded from targeting analysis as it is only observed post-call and would introduce data leakage
- Dashboard is designed for executive consumption with a focus on clarity, accuracy, and actionability
